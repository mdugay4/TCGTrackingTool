What is the goal of TCG Tracking Tool?

Our goal is to provide an easy way for collectors to keep track of the the cards they own. Along with the cards they own, they can also track the changing prices of individual cards. The website also functions as a wishlist for buyers as they can choose to be notified when a card price shifts.

How do I get started?

To get started, you will first have to create an account. Then you will have access to all the website's functions such as adding cards to your portfolio and keeping track of card prices.

How do I change my notification settings?

When you add a card to your inventory, you can also set up to be notified when the card price changes. You can choose to be notified by text, email, or via message on the website. To remove a notification for a card, you can go to your notification settings and remove the notification for the card you no longer want to keep track of.

Where are the icons used on the website from?

All icons can be found at https://icons8.com.

How does TCG player calculate the market price of cards?

The value of a card is based upon actual recent sales from thousands of transactions each day across thousands of individual sellers.
