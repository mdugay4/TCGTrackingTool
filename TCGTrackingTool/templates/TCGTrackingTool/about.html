We are a small senior design team from UNLV. We all have a bit of experience in the trading card game space. We have played in tournaments, bought and sold cards, and have been avid collectors for years. We wanted to build an application that can help people in the trading card game space.


Did you need help building a new deck on a budget?

Just add the cards you want to your inventory and buy them when the price starts to drop.

Did you want to sell off your old stock?

Just add the cards and sell them whenever you are ready.

Are you a merchant who is constantly buying low and selling high?

This tool might be perfect for you!

Do you like collecting cards but don't want to buy the newest ones at full price?

Our application will do all the tracking for you!
